Scope

We aim to help people who are suffering from anxiety disorder specifically psychiatric disorders that involve extreme fear or worry.

Generalized Anxiety Disorder

Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD) is characterized by persistent and excessive worry about a number of different things.

Panic Disorder

Panic disorder is diagnosed in people who experience spontaneous seemingly out-of-the-blue panic attacks and are very preoccupied with the fear of a recurring attack.

Social Anxiety Disorder

intense anxiety or fear of being judged, negatively evaluated, or rejected in a social or performance situation.

Selective Mutism

a severe anxiety disorder where a person is unable to speak in certain social situations, such as with classmates at school or to relatives they do not see very often.

Separation Anxiety

a serious emotional problem characterized by extreme distress when a child is away from the primary caregiver.

Specific Phobias

an overwhelming and unreasonable fear of objects or situations that pose little real danger but provoke anxiety and avoidance.

NCMH

The National Center for Mental Health is dedicated to delivering preventive, curative and rehabilitative mental health care services.

The NCMH is a special training and research hospital mandated to render a comprehensive (preventive, promotive, curative and rehabilitative) range of quality mental health services nationwide.

PMHA

The Philippine Mental Health Association, Inc. (PMHAI) is a private, non-stock organization dedicated to the promotion of mental health and prevention of mental disorders. It is a member of the World Federation for Mental Health.

To advocate for mental health through education, research, provision of community-based holistic and quality mental health services to individuals, families, communities and other stakeholders; and, To lead in the advancement of mental health through partnerships and collaboration with local and international organizations.
